Due to the accident of yesterday, today I decided to take pictures of my car, just as a proof of the damage made by mad freshmen.

A friend of mine has kindly helped with her camera and taking pictures of the car. They are just scary. I just get the feeling that I am living among wild, savage people.

And a neighbour of mine has kindly lent me his Samsung still camera for me to take some extra pictures, before going to the police. It is a Digimax A302, with capacity of 3.2 megapixels.

I guess that, now, the Top Ten Digital Photography Tips will make more sense to me (along with reading the manual of the camera). Oh, by the way, it worked fine with Linux 2.6.16-rc3's mass storage module loaded. No extra drivers needed. Nice toy. And a nice operating system too.
