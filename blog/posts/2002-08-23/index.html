About Me

Tried to go to my university, to see a seminar on the Steiner Problem in Graphs, without success (the traffic didn't let me arrive). Came back home and decided that it would be nice to visit my uncle's store. He was leaving early to catch the last hours of this year's Comdex and I went with him (he gave me an pass).

Its always nice to see all those exposition stands "competing" to see which one has got the prettiest models. And it was also nice to see that flat panels are invading the market, but I wonder how much time it will take before they are "purchaseable", especially for a graduate student.
