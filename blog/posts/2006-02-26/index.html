About Me

Playing with ImageMagick, I discovered that the camera saves the pictures with "quality" settings of 95%, 85%, and 70% when using "super fine", "fine", and "normal" settings for compression, respectively. This answers one of the questions that I had in mind when I started using this camera. Now, the other one is "which resolution to use?".
