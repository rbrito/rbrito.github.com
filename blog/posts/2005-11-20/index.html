About Me

I'm so impressed with the recent improvements in GNU Emacs that I am sincerely considering using it as my "default operating system".

Many people say that joking, but the new features along with a more user-friendly, really make that an interesting option.

I use, by the way, the following extra packages from Debian:

ii  post-el          2004.07.23-2     emacs major mode for editing mail
