Finally, the text that I have been writing for some time now was submitted for the Qualifying Examination of my Master's Degree! This is a landmark in my career and many doors may be open for me now, with many more professional possibilities. It also means that I'll soon be having to present my work.

Went to the shopping mall and later to the supermarket with my sweet girlfriend and bought CDs that I found in the bargain bin for only R$ 2.80 a piece. Got Paul Dianno's solo CD in the bunch.
