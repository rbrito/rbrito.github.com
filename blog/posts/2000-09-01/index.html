About Me

I haven't been having as much time to update my diary as I'd expect to have, but I'm updating it in bursts as I can. Anyway, I strongly suspect the world is keeping a secret conspiration against me. I'm now using some old glasses and I can't see very well. The interesting thing: I didn't even pay all parcels of my new glasses. I just wish I didn't have this huge shortsightedness.
