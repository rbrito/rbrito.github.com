2006-07-11

Had the quite pleasant news that I had my first peer-reviewed work accepted to the CNMAC'2006 Congress (The National Applied and Computational Mathematics Congress).

The work was entitled "Complexidade de Alinhamentos de Seqüências Biológicas" ("Computational Complexity of Biological Sequence Alignments"). This is a quite welcome addition to my curriculum.
